Recipe

  • Recipe 1: Spaghetti Carbonara

    Ingredients: Spaghetti, eggs, pancetta, Parmesan cheese, black pepper.

    Instructions: Cook spaghetti, mix with beaten eggs and cheese, add pancetta, and season with pepper.

  • Recipe 2: Chicken Curry

    Ingredients: Chicken, onions, tomatoes, spices, coconut milk.

    Instructions: Sauté onions, add chicken and spices, stir in tomatoes and coconut milk, simmer until cooked.

  • Recipe 3: Vegetable Stir-fry

    Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, soy sauce, garlic, ginger.

    Instructions: Stir-fry vegetables with garlic and ginger, add soy sauce to taste.

  • Recipe 4: Chocolate Cake

    Ingredients: Flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, butter.

    Instructions: Mix dry ingredients, add wet ingredients, bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.

  • Recipe 5: Caesar Salad

    Ingredients: Romaine lettuce, croutons, Parmesan cheese, Caesar dressing.

    Instructions: Toss lettuce with dressing, add croutons and cheese, serve chilled.

  • Recipe 6: Rice

    Ingredients: Romaine lettuce, croutons, Parmesan cheese, Caesar dressing.

    Instructions: Toss lettuce with dressing, add croutons and cheese, serve chilled.
